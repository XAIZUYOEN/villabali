Villa Irma

  Bali, Seminyak

USD $ 195 per night

What we love

 Cezanne inspected Villa Irma 27 days ago.
  • Option of renting as a 2 or 3 bedroom villa
  • Third bedroom is a studio set-up with a rooftop terrace and private entrance separate from the rest of the villa
  • Studio living space equipped with a home entertainment system: 47" flat screen TV, iPod dockstation & Playstation console
  • Excellent location within Seminyak, only a 10 minute walk from the beach
  • Located within walking distance to Jalan Petitenget and its numerous shops and restaurants
  • Great value for the price

Description

Villa Irma is a stylish and charming retreat within one of Seminyak's most happening areas, Petitenget. Located a quick drive or a 10 minute walk from the beach, you are never too far from a number of top restaurants, beach clubs and spas. Some of Bali's trendiest nightspots are literally down the street, including Potato Head Beach Club and Kudeta. Known especially for its innovative dining scene, be sure to experience the culinary genius of Mamasan, Metis and La Lucciola during your stay in Bali.

Villa Irma is designed with the Balinese open concept in mind, with airy living and dining spaces that overlook the pool, as well as open air showers.The living area features a day bed complete with canopy curtains, a favourite spot to take a nap or lounge with a book and cocktail. This beautiful villa is decked in colours of blue and green, with patterns on the floor tiles simulating little air bubbles in an underwater mermaid's hideout. Guests won't forget they are in Bali, as there is plenty of traditional art that showcases the rich culture of the Island of the Gods.
